Solutions that you actually need

The air at home is clean now

Have you ever wondered about air pollution in your surroundings? - yes you should. If the sensor detects a dangerous level of air pollution, the smart home system is informed in order to perform some preventive actions e.g. system closes all opened windows and turns the air purifier on. Now you can take a deep breath!

Immediate reaction to failure

Inaccurately working electronic devices can cause short circuit or even a fire. Wall Plug gives you plethora of remote features such as switching devices on and off, check on energy consumption and stay informed about any device functioning. Let yourself react instantly by switching the device off via mobile app.

Keep an eye on the kids

Children need lots of sleep in perfect conditions. Now you can be sure that your kid sleeps tight by double checking via camera preview in your mobile app. Air quality in your child's bedroom is becoming a concern nowadays. Keep track of the air quality in the entire house and if any doubts, switch on your air purifier.

Everyone who has a good relationship with their neighbors can benefit of a help hand while being away. Imagine a situation when a suspected activity around your home is detected and your trusted neighbor receives a notification. The neighbor can double check if everything is okay around your property and inform you right after.

Virtual key to your home

The worst case scenario: you are stuck in a traffic jam while your friends are already at your entrance door and need to wait until you get home. Another case is when delivery man is right at the front gate and you're on holiday or at work. Sounds familiar right? Intercom enables you to open front door to people you trust and who need to enter your home at this exact moment. Guests or your kid who forgot his keys won't have to wait for you outside no more. Furthermore, there is no waste of time when courier can leave your parcel inside your premises.

100% safety

Equip your house with a set of smart security devices and create an undisputed safety before the police arrives. Sensors provide data to the alarm system in order to prevent trespassing, robbery attempts or other undesirable activities. This is when a smart home will be visible and audible from a distance thanks to the integrated lights and sound alarm. Deter burglars and set your home into a safety mode that no one wants to experience.

Devices for safe home

Smart Implant

FIBARO Smart Implant is a small, easy to install Z-Wave device that turns standard devices into intelligent ones. It is an ideal, cost-effective solution for those who want to add an alarm system, sensors, thermostats, gates and roller shutters, as well as an audio-video & household appliances to the Z-Wave network.

See the product

Door/Window Sensor 2

FIBARO Door/Window Sensor 2 is a universal, battery-powered proximity sensor that operates using wireless Z-Wave communication. It allows to monitor the closing and opening status of doors and windows. The device can automatically trigger scenes or monitor access to protected rooms.

See the product

Motion Sensor

FIBARO Motion Sensor is a wireless multisensor, which is a motion, temperature and light intensity sensor. The device also detects changes in its own position and tamper attempts. Motion Sensor is battery-powered and updated wirelessly. Its sensitivity can be adjusted to suit the needs of the residents.

See the product

RGBW Controller

Universal module that allows remote control of RGB and RGBW LED strips, halogen lighting and fans. Connected receivers can be powered by 12 or 24 volts. You can easily control the lighting and change the color lights using the mobile app.

See the product

Wall Plug

Practical, portable plug for remote switching on and off electrical appliances and measuring the amount of consumed energy.

See the product

Home Center 2

Brain of FIBARO smart home system which communicates with Plug & Play modules and sensors. Available with a wide catalogue of installed drivers for co-operating devices: cameras, smart TVs, thermostats, alarm panels, media players and control systems.
