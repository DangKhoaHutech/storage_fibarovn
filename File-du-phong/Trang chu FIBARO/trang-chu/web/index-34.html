A bit romantic

Men know how much women like romantic settings during the evenings. However, women can't remember when was the last time their men organized such a wonderful evening for them. We've got a perfect solution to that. One press of a button or a voice command to turn on a romantic music, set up a fireplace and dim the light. All this done simultaneously. Just bring the red wine and you're ready to go!

Fight the Monster Scene

Monsters often appear in the children's bedroom to disturb their while sleeping. Place The Button on the bedside table. After your child sees a monster, the smart home will fight it with soothing lighting under the bed or any other place where monsters usually sneak up. Motion detector will detect any suspicious moves of an intruder. A monster trap of this kind will make your child feel safe.

Ambient garden

Food tastes better outside. A mixture of atmospheric lighting and favorite music selection create a genuine garden party. Keep on grilling your barbecue while opening the sunshade to enjoy the stars, turn on your favorite playlist and garden lamps with a minimalist remote control. It's time to make a toast to a nice evening.

The Zen scene

Everyday rush makes us forget about relaxation among a long task list. Home space often seem to be a place where we should have a time for meditation, however it is far from being calm temple. On the other hand, tuning appropriate light and music together with humidifier purifying the air, we can make it spot where we can close our eyes and free our soul over the peaceful oasis. Take a deep breath...

Let your house inform you about the outside temperature with an appropriate color. By displaying a selection of colors, you will always know which outfit you should wear or if breakfast on the terrace is a good idea. Let your kids know if it's the right time to play outdoor. How simple is that!

Smart home mood management products

The Button

A battery-powered wireless trigger that allows you to control connected devices. Simply activate six actions or preset scenes with one to five clicks or hold down the button. Thanks to its design, it can be mounted literally anywhere you want.

See the product

Dimmer 2

An universal module that regulates the intensity of light and works with different lighting sources. Light dimmer can be used as a dimmer or switch, both in two- and three-wire circuits.

See the product

RGBW Controller

Universal module that allows remote control of RGB and RGBW LED strips, halogen lighting and fans. Connected receivers can be powered by 12 or 24 volts. You can easily control the lighting and change the color lights using the mobile app.

See the product

Motion Sensor

Wireless multisensor, which is a motion, temperature and light intensity sensor. The device also detects changes in its own position and tamper attempts. Motion Sensor is battery-powered and updated wirelessly. Its sensitivity can be adjusted to suit the needs of the residents.

See the product

KeyFob

Battery powered, compact remote control which can always be carried with you as a key ring. Six buttons allow you to control other devices connected Z-Wave network and start scenes created in FIBARO smart home system.

See the product

Home Center 2

Brain of FIBARO smart home system which communicates with Plug & Play modules and sensors. Available with a wide catalogue of installed drivers for co-operating devices: cameras, smart TVs, thermostats, alarm panels, media players and control systems.
