Smart Implant

Make Your Appliances Smarter

Tap into the power of intelligence

The Smart Implant gives your existing devices a whole new level of intelligence. The Smart Implant bestows everyday appliances, such as heating systems, garage doors, lighting, and heating, with a range of cutting-edge functions and features that significantly extend their operation. The Smart Implant is currently the only comprehensive solution available on the market that harnesses the power of Z-Wave technology to transform ordinary and out-of-date appliances into powerful devices that are compatible with the FIBARO Smart Home system.

  • Wireless communication
  • Extending your Z-Wave network
  • Switching your devices on and off
  • Transmitting data between various sensors

Safe to install

Powered by a safe, low-voltage supply that has a range of 9-30V. Protected against any adverse effects associated with an incorrect connection (e.g., incorrect polarity).

Has no negative impact on the main device

Transparent for alarm systems.
Does not interfere with the regular operation of a device.

Many people use a remote control to operate their garage door. If you want the convenience of opening and closing your garage door without having to get out of the car, you can create a remote-control system without having to pay the huge expenses associated with standard automation solutions. Simply plug the Smart Implant into the garage door controller to connect the gate with the FIBARO system, and you can then perform all the actions you need using your KeyFob.

Quickly and easily integrate your wired alarm system with the FIBARO home management system thanks to the installation of Smart Implant. The Smart Implant extends the functionality of motion sensors and opening doors/windows by incorporating them into the smart home system. What’s more, with the Smart Implant, you can activate the system from remote locations using just your smartphone. You can be sure that your property and possessions are secure and can verify the status of the alarm system no matter where you are.

One of the areas of the home that can benefit the most from automation is the heating system. Placing the FIBARO Smart Implant in the controller of your furnace and underfloor heating brings a new level of intelligent heating to your home. Once the implant is installed, you can change the heating schedules wherever you are using just a smartphone or tablet. In fact, if you prefer, you can leave FIBARO to completely manage your heating system on your behalf. FIBARO can completely control the heating system for you according to your predefined temperature requirements, thereby ensuring that your home always remains at the temperature you want.

You’ve got an intelligent home, so why not create an intelligent garden as well? The Smart Implant is ideal for gardening enthusiasts who have equipped their garden with wind, temperature, and soil moisture sensors. Once the Smart Implant is incorporated into these sensors, they are equipped with the ability to communicate with the FIBARO system, which collects the transmitted information and performs the necessary steps on your behalf to ensure your garden receives the required heat and water at precisely the right time. You can even use it to maintain the heat of your swimming pool!

We often have valuable devices and appliances at home that we want to keep because of their sentimental value or unique design. Thanks to the Smart Implant, devices that have been around for years but are out of date, such as AV-receivers, radios, amplifiers and projectors, can continue to serve your needs and add even more value by becoming key components of your intelligent home system. For example, after installing the Smart Implant in your audio equipment, you can turn it on using voice commands. Enjoy your favorite equipment in a new, smart version.

The FIBARO system can get your devices working for you with minimal effort. By installing the Smart Implant in a wall switch and your selected equipment, you can create a ‘Home theatre’ ambiance with just the flick of a switch or a simple hand gesture. Within seconds, the light will be dimmed, the projector and music will turn on, and the blinds will close. Sit back and enjoy the film. The Smart Implant is small but extremely versatile.

A device for special tasks

The Smart Implant is the outcome of extensive studies that aimed to extend the functionality of the universal binary sensor. Through analyzing the needs of thousands of FIBARO installers and system users, we created the Smart Implant. The Smart Implant is a real game changer; it allows you to integrate your ‘non-smart’ devices with the FIBARO System and provides installers with a solution for our customers’ installation problems.

Control home appliances

  • Remote control devices
  • Assign up to 12 possible actions to one scene

Supporting sensors

  • Compatible with binary and 0-10 V sensors
  • Inbuilt temperature sensor
  • Supports up to 6 Dallas DS18B20 temperature sensors or one DHT-22 temperature and humidity sensor
