A sense of design

The FIBARO Flood Sensor achieves the perfect balance between aesthetic and function. Featuring a sleek, artistic design, the FIBARO device combines state-of-the-art technology with a harmonious form.

Multi-functional

The FIBARO Flood Sensor does much more than sense water leakage. It incorporates cutting-edge technologies that are designed to make your life easier.

  • Leak monitor

  • Temperature monitor

  • Antifraud technology

  • Sound alarm

  • Telescopic stands

  • Light indicator

Fully compatible with HomeKit

We have developed a bespoke series of the FIBARO Flood Sensors that are specifically designed to work with Apple HomeKit. These devices offer all the great features of the regular sensors that were created for use with the FIBARO System. Learn more.

Safety

Solutions you can count on

Smart notifications

The FIBARO system will immediately send a notification to your smartphone as soon as a leak, flood, or tampering attempt is detected.

Buoyant

Stops water supply upon leak detection

The system is supplied with innovative electro valves that immediately cut off the water supply upon leak detection in order to minimize damage. Wherever you are, you can have confidence your home is protected.

Always on duty

Smart Security

The state-of-the-art device can detect minor threats, such as small leaks, before they become major issues. Combine the remarkable abilities of the FIBARO system with the cutting-edge functions of the FIBARO Flood Sensor to access a level of home security you have never previously experienced.

Effortlessly manage floor heating

The integrated temperature monitor allows the FIBARO Flood Sensor to manage floor heating. Simply place the device on any surface to measure its temperature. The FIBARO system will automatically adjust the temperature in response to the information it receives from the FIBARO Flood Sensor.

Award-winning technology

The FIBARO combines seamless design with the latest technologies. It has received a wide range of accolades and awards for technical solutions and design.

 

Choose your device

There are two versions of the FIBARO Flood Sensor available: One for use with the FIBARO system and the second for use with Apple HomeKit. If you have the FIBARO system or an alternative system that is based on Z-Wave, choose the Z-Wave version. If you need a device that is compatible with Apple HomeKit, choose the Apple HomeKit version.
